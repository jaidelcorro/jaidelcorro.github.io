Jaime del Corro is a graphic designer working between Madrid and London. He is interested in the use of design and research as critical tools that unpack the complexities of living in a high-tech world. His work ranges from commisions by clients, artists and collectives to self-initiated research projects and cross-disciplinary collaborations.

He graduated from MA GMD in LCC in 2019. Since 2022 he is a support teacher at IED in Madrid. He is also a part-time designer at This Side Up. Previously worked at Zak Group, Spassky-Fischer, and N-E.
